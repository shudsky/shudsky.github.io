PORTFOLIO

Mount Roller Dispatch

A game created for the 2023 iteration of Ubisoft Montreal's Game Lab competition, which has students across universities in Quebec compete to create the best game prototype in 10 weeks. The theme of the 2023 edition was "Arcade". I served as an artist and contributed to graphic design and 3D modeling.

Hiding in Plain Sight

A detective game about figuring out people's identities and solving the mysterious dissappearance of a girl.

It was created in a team of four for the 2022 GamerELLA game jame over the span of 48 hours; I served as programmer and assisted in narrative design.

Amsonia: Oblivion Unbound

A Skyrim-inspired work of interactive fiction created using Twine.

It was created in a team of two in 2021 over the span of five weeks, with myself serving primarily as programmer as well as assisting in narrative design.

Colourdle

A Wordle-like where you have five attempts to guess the RGB values of a randomly generated colour.

It was programmed entirely by me in May 2022.

Frogger

A mini version of the classic game Frogger, created entirely in Java.

It was created by me over the span of about 8 weeks to help with learning Java.

Stranger Things Paint

A small painting program themed around the Netflix show Stranger Things, created in python and pygame.

It was created by me over the span of about 8 weeks to help with learning python.

Class Website

This is a website I created in the course CART 211: Creative Computing and Network Culture. The site was regularly updated throughout the duration of the semester as a sort of reflection on the week's lessons. I liked the style so much that I've adapted it to my personal website here.

It was done entirely by me over the course of the 12 week semester.

PS - click the lightbulb at the side!

Class Final Project

Also done in CART 211 as the culminating final project was this website/piece of interactive media. It was created in response to one of the readings we did in the course, On Software, or the Persistence of Visual Knowledge by Wendy Hui Kyong Chun.
Playable by typing what is displayed (minus the ">" symbol) - alternately, just hit the left arrow key and it will auto-fill the answers.

It was programmed and written entirely by me over the course of about four weeks.
